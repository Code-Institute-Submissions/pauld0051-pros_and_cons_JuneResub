  • Reporting a user or a post

    Your safety and wellbeing are our priority and we offer a number of easy tools for you to use to protect yourself from bullying, harassment, hate-speech, spam and more.

        Set your questions to "Friends Only" () that way only people you know will be able to see your posts.

        Unfriend () users who are harassing or bullying you.

        Report an incident immediately by filling out a short message. Your administrator will investigate within 2 working days.

    The best protection against bullying and harassment is not to retaliate. Most bullies are attention seekers, and you giving them that attention feeds their need for attention. Ignoring them takes away any power they might think they have.

    If you or someone you know or another user appears to be in danger, do not keep it secret, tell someone you trust immediately and report it as soon as possible to us.


  • Adding a question

    You can add any range of questions that you want to check. Be specific in your title but more general in your text about the question. The more specific you are about a topic, the more replies you're likely to get. Click on the question mark symbol () at the top of any page to ask question.

        Ask questions that have clear pros and cons associated.

        Be clear about what you're asking, and edit your question if replies don't seem to be targeting your question.

    Pros and Cons is about being social and getting the answers to difficult questions or just a push in the right direction. Make sure you get involved with answering questions with pros and cons too, that way you'll get a real feel for how the site works.


  • Adding a pro or a con

    You can submit a pro and/or con to any post that is open to you (either by your friends or public posts). Simply add your comment to either a pro or a con and submit. This will open the same question in the browser and allow you to add more pros or cons to the question. The more you're involved, the more people will become involved in your posts.

        Give specific replies and be careful not to repeat other people's responses.

        Give personal experience, anecdote, or even an evidence based approach to your responses.

    Users who ask questions can then weigh up the respective pros and cons to determine their best course of action.


  • Cancelling your account

    You can cancel your account at anytime by sending us a message here. Of course we don't want to see you go, but your administrator will help remove your account manually. This is done to avoid accidental deletion and will take about 2 working days to complete. It is your responsibility to delete your posts. They do not go when you go. If they remain, and someone joins with that identical name, they will have control over your posts. You can simply delete your posts by clicking the delete button on each question you wrote. You can do this from your profile () page.

        We'll be sorry to see you go, but you are welcome to join back up again at anytime.

        Let us also know what we could have done to improve your experience.

    Currently you can not delete your pros or cons that you replied to within someone's question. If you really need one of those removed, let us know which post and which reply you want removed. This can be actioned within two working days.
